Back From The Break!

  • Gursimmer Banwait
  • January 16, 2022

Hardware

As a member of the team, I am ecstatic to share the progress we've made on our revolutionary device! The mechanical design is coming along swimmingly and we're in the final stages of perfecting the full device case. We're also making sure that the design is 3D printable and user-friendly by making it as thin as possible.

We're stacking up some amazing features, like a camera MUX, RPi 0, RPi Sugar, and a battery, all while keeping the device sleek and slim - shorter than your average smartphone. Plus, we're incorporating a torsion spring clip design modeled after an off-the-shelf product for easy use.

But that's not all, the gooseneck design is where it's at! We're taking inspiration from an item by Bedi, but making it even better by making the inside hollow for harness passthrough. We're also thinking about using a break-out board to convert ribbon cable to small gauge wires for a more efficient design. And the lamp design? It's going to blow your mind! We're making it user-centric so that users can curl it to face down at an angle, not just orthogonal. Plus, we're adding a simple LED on/off feature that's always on and attached to the central bank.

In terms of the electrical design, we're utilizing a ribbon cable for the camera(s) and considering curling it inside the gooseneck for a sleek finish. The LED PCB is also a work of art, feeding off the plated thru-holes for power and ground connections.

Software

The software side is just as impressive, our Bluetooth code is functioning smoothly, and we're adding a one-button power on feature for searching known devices. Plus, we're implementing a watchdog timer for power on and off to avoid always checking for pairing.

We're also exploring the use of NRCLex, but we're not settling for anything less than perfection, we're open to train our own model if needed. And the cherry on top? We're planning the UI using Figma to make it visually stunning.

Overall, this device is going to be a game-changer and I can't wait for everyone to see it!
