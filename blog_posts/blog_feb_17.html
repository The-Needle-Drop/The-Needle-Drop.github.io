Bringing NeedleDrop to Life - All Systems Go!

  • Jacob Piirsalu
  • February 17, 2023

We're excited to announce that the NeedleDrop iOS app is officially complete! After several weeks of hard work, our team has been able to bring to life all the necessary features to make the user's reading experience an all-encompassing and enjoyable one.

A "Swift" User Experience

Our primary focus for this final week was the implementation of several new features to the iOS app, including full Spotify API integration. With this implementation, our sentiment analysis engine can query for songs that fit the mood of the book a user currently reading, and play them directly from the app. The playback controls for music have also been added, and music can now be played in the background for a seamless experience.

We've also integrated the sentiment detection code from last week into the iOS app. The app is now able to detect the novel's emotions as they read and play songs accordingly. This feature helps the user to better connect with the book they're reading and engage with the music and literature more effectively!

Additionally, I've created a connectivity sheet view (swipe up/down menu) to show the connectivity status of the NeedleDrop device. This sheet view is a crucial addition as it provides the user with real-time updates on the device's connectivity status, ensuring a seemless user experience!

Addicted to Learning

To further incentivize users to continue their reading streak, I've added User Metrics tracking with a graph view. This feature allows users to track their reading frequency and duration, giving them a visual representation of their progress.

We're thrilled to have completed the iOS app development and to have implemented all the necessary features for a successful user experience. With this level of integration and functionality, the NeedleDrop device is now one step closer to being in the hands of our users. We can't wait to see the impact it will have on the way people read and engage with music in the future!
